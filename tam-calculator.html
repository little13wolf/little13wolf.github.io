Valorant Team Average Max Calculator

This program is intended to help you check your team's average player score to ensure you meet the division requirements for the Galorants NA League. Input your players' rank information and this will tell you the team score. If you have substitutes, enter their information as well to calculate your team's MAX and MIN scores.

If you have an incomplete team, this will tell you your team's current score foe the number of players entered.

Please Note: If you enter 3 main players and 2 substitutes, the program will treat the subs like your last 2 main players, so your "Main Team Score" will be off.
